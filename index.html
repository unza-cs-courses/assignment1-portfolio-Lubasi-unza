About Me

I am a passionate and hard-working student enrolled at The University of Zambia (UNZA). I am currently in my 4th and final year of my Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Computer Science with a Major in Software Engineering. As a student taught and learning Web Programming Technologies, I aim to utilise the skills obtained in the duration of this course for practical and commercially viable applications.

I have been interested in technology and computers since I was very young. While not the science fiction I grew with as a child, I became increasingly interested in technology as my knowledge grew. In my secondary school years, particularly mid secondary school I gained a greater interest in how websites worked both on their front and back end. I would make simple websites on my own for practice and enjoyment, which while attending university aided me refining my abilities and motivation.

I am currently developing my knowledge of HTML according to the official HTML5 standard, CSS and JavaScript. All these languages are essential tools to develop user friendly, efficient, responsive websites. Aside from knowledge of how to simply use the tools, I have gained the technical knowledge to flesh out and make the sites intuitive and user-friendly though practise.

I enjoy solving problems and transforming ideas into functional digital products that can utilised on a variety different devices including computers, tablets, and the ubiquitous mobile platform. Responsive design and accessibility are especially important as in our modern world products should be able to be utilised by customers of various abilities, devices, and backgrounds.

I hope I can effectively utilise the skills learnt through my education to produce effective and most viable products and solutions, that contribute positively and meaningfully to the technology sector.
